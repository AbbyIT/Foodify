A Little Joke To Brighten Your Day

The IngrediantFy App helps users discover new recipes, plan their meals, and manage their diets whilst throwing in a little bit of laughter along the way for a healthier lifestyle.

The App enables users to view healthy for the soul jokes at the touch of a button and to search for recipes based on ingredients that they have in their pantry.
It allows users to plan their meals, save recipes, check calorie counts and generate shopping lists.
Users can check their fridges for left over ingredients our App will then enable them to make a meal out of the ingredients in their fridges or kitchen cupboards!

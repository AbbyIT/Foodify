Foodify App



Foodify App help users discover new recipes, plan their meals, and manage their diets. The App enable users to search for recipes based on ingredients, dietary restrictions, and cuisine.

It allow users to plan their meals for the week, save recipes, and generate shopping lists. Users can check their fridges for left over ingredients our App will then enable them to make a meal out of the ingredients in their fridges or kitchen cupboards!

The App provides Nutritional information: Provide information about the calorie count, macronutrient breakdown, and other important nutritional information for each recipe.

*Click the SEARCH option to see the full features our App offers*. With our easy to use and informative App, you are sure to get all the information you need about consuming healthy calories in one place!

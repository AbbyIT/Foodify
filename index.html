Foodify App



This App allow users to search for a particular dish. It then provides 5 varieties of dishes that matched the requested dish. It shows preparation time, calories per serving and ingredients in recipe

The App will help people that love to cook and conscious of their calories intake. The App will can be used as part of a healthy dieting plan for single people as well as families.
